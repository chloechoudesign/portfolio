Starring You® AI Case Study

A personalized AI experience designed to scale delight, engagement, and subscriptions at JibJab.

Overview

In 2024, we launched Starring You® AI to modernize JibJab’s iconic personalization feature using AI-generated face swapping. We began with an app-first release, iterated quickly, and later expanded to web.

My Role

  • Led UX and product design strategy
  • Collaborated with AI/ML and engineering teams
  • Designed core user flows and interfaces for mobile and web

Key Challenges

  • Legacy cropping UX was slow and outdated
  • Needed to scale high-quality face swaps across packs
  • Boost conversion without compromising fun

Process

  • User research & legacy audit
  • Mobile-first wireframes and prototyping
  • Collaboration with ML to integrate Stable Diffusion
  • Usability testing and iteration

Core Flows

  • Photo Upload & Auto-Crop
  • Pack Selection & Preview
  • AI-Powered Generation
  • Result Sharing & Download
  • Subscription Upsell
